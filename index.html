02 — The framework

Six lifecycle stages.
One funnel.

Every enquiry lives at a specific point in the journey from stranger to student to alumni. Each stage needs its own voice, its own offer, and its own sequence.

STAGE · 01
Cold Lead
First contact from a paid ad or organic search. No relationship yet. The job is curiosity and trust.
STAGE · 02
Abandoned Application
Started the form and dropped. One of the biggest conversion levers hiding in any admissions funnel.
STAGE · 03
Pre-Enrolment Push
Offer made, deposit pending, intake approaching. Urgency without panic.
STAGE · 04
Pre-Start Welcome
Enrolled but not yet started. Reduce no-show rate. Build belonging before day one.
STAGE · 05
Re-engagement
Lapsed leads from previous intakes. Warm data sitting in HubSpot. Reactivate for the next cohort.
STAGE · 06
Alumni Upsell
Graduates moving to the next qualification. Lowest cost, highest lifetime value segment in any college.
